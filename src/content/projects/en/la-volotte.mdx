An e-commerce website for a lovely Wool shop in Aachen, Germany. La Volotte is a one-stop-shop for all your wool needs and has a wide variety of wool from sustainable sources.

Built with Astro, TailwindCSS and Snipcart for the shoppingcart. 

Key project features:
- Masonry layout with intelligent image clustering
- Lazy-loading image system with blur placeholders
- Simple product listing to enable frequent product updates
- SEO optimized with optimized images and sitemap
- Custom filtering for the products

The result is an elegant digital showcase that effectively represents the shops creative identity while offering an intuitive platform for client engagement.
